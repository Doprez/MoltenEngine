Molten.Graphics.LightData

A vertex structure for storing capsule light data.

Fields  
Color3 The light color, as 3 floats.
Forward The forward direction of the capsule light.
HalfLength The half-length of the capsule light.
Intensity The light intensity.
Length The length of the capsule length.
Position The light position.
RangeRcp The reciprocal light range.
TessFactor The tessellation factor. A Factor of 0 will disable the light.
Transform The light transform.
