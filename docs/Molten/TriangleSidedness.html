Molten.TriangleSidedness

Sidedness of a triangle or mesh. A triangle can be double sided, or allow one of its sides to let interacting objects through.

Fields  
Clockwise The triangle will interact with objects from which the winding of the triangle appears to be clockwise.
Counterclockwise The triangle will interact with objects from which the winding of the triangle appears to be counterclockwise..
DoubleSided The triangle will interact with objects coming from both directions.
